Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Learn more about how we collect and treat your personal information.

According to data protection regulations, we have created the following privacy policy to describe how we collect and use personal information about our users. We commit to using your personal information in accordance with our responsibilities.  

Who we are and how can you contact us?  

We are a campaign group (no formal name) campaigning to save New Mills Leisure Centre Facilities. We are a group of individuals (all leisure centre users) who have informally joined forces to put pressure on High Peak Borough Council to find out, listen to, and respect the needs of the real users of New Mills Leisure Centre. We can be contacted via info@nmlc-campaign.net.

Why do we need your information? 

We only collect information volunteered by website visitors in order to be able to contact them with updates about the ‘Save New Mills Leisure Centre Facilities’ campaign. For example we plan to contact people when the High Peak Borough Council announces their consultation so that they can fully participate.

How do we collect your information? 

We will ask for your consent to send you updates.  

We only collect personal information that enables us to email you (name and email address).

We will ensure your data is only used for the purpose it was requested and warrant your data is stored securely.  

How do we use your information? 

To provide you with email updates about the ‘Save New Mills Leisure Centre Facilities’ campaign.

How do we store your information and for how long? 

We store your data securely behind a password protected website platform. When you submit your personal data, you agree to its storing or processing. We make sure to take all steps reasonably necessary to ensure that your data is treated securely and in accordance with this privacy policy. 

Your information is kept for no longer than necessary for the purposes for which it was collected, taking into account guidance from the Information Commissioner’s Office.  

Do you share my information with anyone else? 

Only administrators of our website with the highest administrative level will be able to access your data. Administrators will only use your data for sending emails.

We do not share your data with anyone else.

How can I change, delete or ask to see my information? 

If you want to stop receiving our emails, you can opt out at any time by clicking on the unsubscribe button at the bottom of any of our emails.    

If you think we have personal information that needs to be changed, please contact us (info@nmlc-campaign.net) to let us know what you think it is and we will do everything we can to rectify it. 

If you want to know full details of the information we have on record about you, do not hesitate to request it.  

If you would like us to delete all the information we have on record about you, please contact us via the email address above.  

How to find out more, or make a complaint about our approach to data protection? 

If you would like to update your details, would like more information or have any questions about this policy, please contact us on info@nmlc-campaign.net. 

To make a formal complaint about our approach to data protection or raise privacy concerns directly with us, please contact: 

Save New Mills Leisure Centre Facilities Campaign

If you feel that your data has not been handled correctly, or you are unhappy with our response to any requests you have made to us regarding the use of your personal data, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner’s Office. 

You can contact them by phone or live chat at https://ico.org.uk/global/contact-us/  

Or make a complaint here https://ico.org.uk/make-a-complaint/ (external links open in a new window; please note we can’t be responsible for the content of external websites).
